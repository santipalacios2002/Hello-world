PinkFong

Bio

Pinkfong (Korean: 핑크퐁) is the children's educational brand of SmartStudy, a South Korean educational entertainment company. Pinkfong consists of mainly children's songs, of which the most famous is "Baby Shark"; with its dance video receiving over 7.8 billion views on YouTube, it is the most-viewed video on the site. Their educational channel has more than 43 million subscribers, with a brightly-colored programming of stories, sing-along songs and dances represented by a pink fox named "Pinkfong". The global product development company has more than 4,000 children's videos, songs, games and apps.
